Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of HDPE pipes in infrastructure?

HDPE pipes are designed to transport water, gas, and other fluids with precision, allowing for complex routing that is essential in modern infrastructure and manufacturing processes.

What are the benefits of using HDPE pipes over metal pipes?

Lightweight, corrosion-resistant, excellent chemical resistance, longer service life (50+ years), leak-proof fusion joints, and significantly lower installation costs.

How are HDPE pipes manufactured?

Through a high-precision extrusion process where PE100 grade material is melted and forced through a die, then cooled, sized, marked, and cut to specification under strict quality control.

What are the common applications of HDPE pipes?

Water distribution, irrigation, gas distribution, industrial fluid handling, mining, telecommunications conduits, and sewerage systems.

Can HDPE pipes be customized?

Yes. We offer full customization including diameter (20mm–1600mm), wall thickness, pressure rating, coil length, and surface finish to meet your exact project requirements.
